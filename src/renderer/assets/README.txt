Place your Blackstar logo image here.

Recommended size:  256x256 px or larger for sharp display

The app will use this image in the header and home screen.
For the Windows taskbar/executable icon, also place an icon file:
  icon.ico  (256x256, multi-resolution recommended)

After adding your files, the app will automatically use them.
